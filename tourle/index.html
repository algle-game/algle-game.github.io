Tourle

In Tourle, you must find a shortest roundtrip (tour) visiting all nodes. You can draw the tour by adding edges between nodes. To add an edge, click the corresponding nodes. On click, the node will be marked blue. If the same node is clicked again, it will be deselected. To remove an edge, simply click on the edge. If your tour is valid (every node has degree two, and it is one single tour), you can press the "Check" button. This will show you, how far away you are from an optimal tour (in percent over the optimal tour length).
